Product Evolution

2022

Early Adopter

Recognizing the emerging trend of AI and the potential of LLM, launched AI Writer in Aug. 2022—4 months before ChatGPT—establishing a strong first-mover advantage.

2023

Exploring AI Potentials

Developed 10+ apps, including AI Keyboard, AI Tutor, Soulmate AI, and xTwin, experimenting with AI use cases across productivity, social interaction, and companionship.

2024

Multimodal, Long Context

Google launched multimodal Gemini with a 1M-token context, we recognized its potential for note-taking and summarization, leading us to build AI Notebook (HyNote).

2025

Scaling & Hardware

Growing HyNote to 600K users with $2M in sales, and developing our first AI Hardware: HyBran Voice Recorder.

2026

Personal Assistant & Agents

We will merge AI agents with hardware, delivering deeply personalized assistants that predict user needs and handle tasks autonomously.
